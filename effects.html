Cyberbullying can take various forms but no matter what form they are, intentions are all malicious.


Effects

Cyberbullying could cause victims to suffer emotional distress, anxiety, and depression.

It leads to a decline in self-esteem, academic struggles, and even physical health issues.

The impact extends to strained relationships, behavioral changes, and thoughts of suicide.


Solutions

Education and Awareness
  • Implement awareness campaigns to educate about cyberbullying consequences.

    • Social Media Responsibility
  • Encourage social media companies to implement anti-bullying tools.

    • Legal Measures
  • Law enforcement to investigate and take legal action against serious cyberbullying cases.
  • This involvement may result in criminal charges.
